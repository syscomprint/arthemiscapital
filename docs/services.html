Our Advisory Services

Arthemis Capital advises clients on raising capital, executing transactions and structuring complex financing solutions.

01

Capital Raising

Arthemis Capital advises clients on raising debt and equity financing from international investors, banks and development finance institutions.

Our capital raising capabilities include:

  • Senior debt financing
  • Subordinated debt
  • Structured financing solutions
  • Export Credit Agency (ECA) financing
  • Equity capital raising

The firm structures financing solutions aligned with project economics, regulatory environments and investor expectations.

02

Mergers & Acquisitions

Arthemis Capital advises clients on strategic acquisitions, divestitures and corporate transactions.

Our services include:

  • Buy-side advisory
  • Sell-side advisory
  • Strategic partnerships and joint ventures
  • Transaction structuring and negotiation

We support clients throughout the entire M&A process from strategic evaluation to transaction closing.

03

Structured & Project Finance

Arthemis Capital structures complex financing solutions supporting infrastructure development and industrial investment.

Capabilities include:

  • EPC + Finance solutions
  • Export credit agency financing
  • Structured debt transactions

Our expertise allows clients to mobilise long-term capital for large-scale projects across emerging markets.
