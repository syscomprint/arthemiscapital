Services

Independent advisory across the full lifecycle of strategic financial transactions.

Arthemis Capital operates across sectors including Financial Institutions, Infrastructure, Energy and Resources, Strategic Corporates.

Capital Raising

Arthemis Capital advises clients on raising debt and equity financing from international investors, banks and development finance institutions.

Mergers & Acquisitions

Arthemis Capital advises clients on strategic acquisitions, divestitures and corporate transactions, including buy-side advisory, sell-side advisory, strategic partnerships and joint ventures.

Structured & Project Finance

Arthemis Capital structures complex financing solutions supporting infrastructure development and industrial investment, including project finance structuring, EPC + Finance solutions and structured debt transactions.

Strategic & Sovereign Advisory

Arthemis Capital provides strategic financial advisory to governments, financial institutions and corporates across financial strategy, investment structuring, institutional capital mobilisation and strategic partnerships.

Restructuring

Arthemis Capital advises clients facing financial restructuring challenges, including debt restructuring, liability management, capital structure optimisation and financial recovery strategies.

PPP Finance

Arthemis Capital advises governments and private sector partners on Public-Private Partnership (PPP) financing structures, including PPP structuring, financial modelling, investor engagement and project financing.

Trade Finance

Arthemis Capital structures trade financing solutions supporting banks and corporates engaged in international trade, including structured trade finance, commodity financing and banking trade facilities.
