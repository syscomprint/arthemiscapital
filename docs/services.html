Services

Independent advisory across the full lifecycle of strategic financial transactions.

Arthemis Capital advises clients on raising capital, executing transactions and structuring complex financing solutions aligned with project economics, regulatory environments and investor expectations.

01

Capital Raising

Arthemis Capital advises clients on raising debt and equity financing from international investors, banks and development finance institutions. Capabilities include senior debt financing, subordinated debt, structured financing solutions, export credit agency financing and equity capital raising.

02

Mergers & Acquisitions

Independent advice on acquisitions, divestitures and corporate transactions, including buy-side advisory, sell-side advisory, strategic partnerships, joint ventures, transaction structuring and negotiation support from evaluation through closing.

03

Structured & Project Finance

Complex financing solutions supporting infrastructure development and industrial investment, including project finance structuring, EPC + Finance solutions, export credit agency financing and structured debt transactions.

04

Strategic Advisory

Strategic financial advisory to governments, financial institutions and corporates across financial strategy, investment structuring, institutional capital mobilisation and strategic partnerships.

05

Restructuring

Advice on debt restructuring, liability management, capital structure optimisation and financial recovery strategies for clients facing complex restructuring challenges.

06

PPP Finance

Support for governments and private sector partners on public-private partnership financing structures, including PPP structuring, financial modelling, investor engagement and project financing.

07

Trade Finance

Structured trade finance, commodity financing and banking trade facilities for banks and corporates engaged in international trade.
