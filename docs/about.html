About Arthemis Capital

Firm overview, mission, vision and geographic reach.

Independent financial advisory across Sub-Saharan Africa and other growth markets.

Arthemis Capital Ltd is an independent financial advisory firm registered in the United Kingdom in 2020. The firm specialises in capital raising, mergers & acquisitions and complex financial structuring across Sub-Saharan Africa and other growth markets.

From its London base, Arthemis Capital advises sovereign governments, financial institutions, corporates and investors seeking access to international capital and strategic investment opportunities.

The firm combines deep financial structuring expertise with strong relationships across global investors, development finance institutions and international lenders.

Mission

To mobilise global capital and deliver strategic financial solutions that support economic growth, infrastructure development and institutional strengthening across emerging markets.

Vision

To become a trusted financial advisory platform connecting international investors with high-impact opportunities across Africa and other growth economies.

What We Do

Arthemis Capital advises clients on the structuring and execution of strategic financial transactions.

Our work spans the full transaction lifecycle, including financial structuring, investor engagement, negotiation support and execution.

Geographic Reach

Arthemis Capital operates primarily across Sub-Saharan Africa while maintaining strong access to international capital markets from its London base.

This international positioning allows Arthemis Capital to bridge global capital with high-growth investment opportunities across emerging markets.
